Chinmay Ram

Hi! I’m Chinmay Ram — a solo game designer and level designer working in Unity 3D. I specialize in crafting immersive levels, configuring gameplay interactions using the Unity Inspector, and designing horror and experimental games. I work without writing code, using prefab logic, visual tools, and custom scripts to bring my ideas to life. My work draws inspiration from psychological horror, creating atmospheric, unsettling experiences that linger with players. Explore my projects to see my expertise in level design and horror game development.
